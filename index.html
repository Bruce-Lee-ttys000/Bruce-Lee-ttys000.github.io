Brief Biography

Shuyang Li is a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). He is supervised by Prof. Qiang Wu, Chief Scientist of the National Key R&D Program, doctoral advisor, and second-level professor (currently a Qiushi Distinguished Professor at Zhejiang University). His co-supervisor is Prof. Ran Wang, a doctoral advisor and professor at NUAA.

To date, he has published 13 papers in CCF-recommended journals and conferences as first author, co-first author, or co-author. Among these, 7 are first-author papers, 4 are in CCF-A/B recommended list, and 8 are in Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) Journal Division Q1/Q2. For a complete list of his publications, please refer to his Google Scholar page. He also serves as a reviewer for several leading journals in computer networking, including IEEE/ACM TON, IEEE TMC, and IEEE TWC. He attended and gave an oral presentation at IEEE WCNC 2025 in Milan, Italy, which is one of the major international conferences in the field of communications (the other two flagship IEEE conferences are ICC and GLOBECOM).
