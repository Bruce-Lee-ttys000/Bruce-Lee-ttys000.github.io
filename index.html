Brief Biography

Shuyang Li is a Ph.D. candidate at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA). He is supervised by Prof. Qiang Wu, Chief Scientist of the National Key R&D Program, doctoral advisor, and Qiushi Distinguished Professor at Zhejiang University. His co-supervisor is Prof. Ran Wang, a doctoral advisor and professor at NUAA.

To date, he has published 13 papers in CCF-recommended journals and conferences as first author, co-first author, or co-author. Among these, 7 are first-author papers, 4 are in the CCF-A/B recommended list, and 7 are in the Q1 / Q2 of journals according to the Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS) Journal Ranking (中科院一区/二区). He attended and delivered an oral presentation at IEEE WCNC 2025 in Milan, Italy, one of the three flagship international conferences in communications alongside IEEE ICC and IEEE GLOBECOM. He also serves as a reviewer for several leading journals in computer networking, including IEEE/ACM TON, IEEE TMC, and IEEE TWC.

I am currently seeking postdoctoral opportunities related to non-terrestrial networks, edge intelligence, and AGIC networks at institutions outside mainland China. If you are reading this message, the information is still valid. Please feel free to contact me for potential collaboration via email.
